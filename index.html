The Big Idea

What if your AI assistant could actually do things — not just talk, but think, remember, and act on your behalf?

🧠

An Assistant, Not a Chatbot

Siri talks. Alexa listens. Jeniphyr acts. It books things, handles email, controls your home, searches the web, manages your files, and remembers everything you tell it.

💬

Talk From Anywhere

Text Jeniphyr from your phone through Telegram. No apps to download, no dashboards to open. Just send a message and get a response. Works from your couch, a coffee shop, or another country.

✋

It Learns You

Jeniphyr remembers your preferences, your contacts, your routines. "Book my usual" actually works because it knows what your usual is. The more you use it, the smarter it gets.

🤝

Specialists That Delegate

Behind the scenes, multiple assistants collaborate. Jeniphyr routes your request to the right specialist — research, dev, monitoring. They share memories and delegate to each other automatically.
