Two Ways to Run It

Choose the setup that fits your comfort level.

Free — Self-Hosted

Run It Yourself

Install on your own server or Mac. Full control.

  • All Hands available
  • Direct local access

  • You manage updates
Hosted Account

We Run It For You

Sign up, plug in your AI key, start chatting.

  • Cloud Hands work instantly
  • Optional local agent for home access
  • We handle hosting/updates
  • Your AI key, your costs

BYOAI — Bring Your Own AI Key
